APPE-TEASERS

INGREDIENTS

JELLIED TOMATO MADRILENE

  • 2 tablespoons unflavored gelatin
  • 2 and a quarter cups tomato juice
  • 3 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated onion
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • Dash of pepper

LEMON SOUP

  • 1 can condensed chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

STEPS

JELLIED TOMATO MADRILENE

  1. Sprinkle gelatin on tomato juice to soften
  2. Dissolve bouillon cubes in water; stir into gelatin mixture until gelatin is dissolved
  3. Stir in onion, salt and pepper
  4. Refrigerate until set

LEMON SOUP

  1. Measure all ingredients into small saucepan
  2. Beat with rotary beater until blended
  3. Heat just to boiling over low heat, stirring constantly to prevent curdling
